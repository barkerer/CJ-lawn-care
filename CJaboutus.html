Our History

We started back in 2009 when i was sixteen years old mowing yards and shoveling snow for 16$ an hour. I then started working for a landscaping company for a few years learning the business. This is where I became an expert on doing various landscaping tasks such as mowing lawns, building retaining walls and rock features, trimming bushes, hedges and trees and even installing sod and planting grass. After that I decided I would start a business of my own in Port Angeles, WA. (Which for those of you that dont know its a little north of Seattle WA). A few years later because of family I decided to move my business to Spokane, WA and restart it here. We are still small and homegrown but yet very strong and passionate enough about our business to make every job perfect. We only hire the best and most trustworthy employees as that is the face of our company. Today we are still operated by the same business ethics we had back when we first became a company which is loyalty, integrity, and passion.

Meet the owner

As the founding father of my business, I Colby Justice started this idea when I was sixteen years old mowing lawns and shoveling driveways and porches. As stated above I grew up in Port Angeles, WA and started working at a young age to help out my family. After working in landscaping for years, I decided I wanted to finally become my own boss and start a buisness for myself. Enter CJ Lawn Care. From this business I hope to have a legacy to pass down upon my two sons and daughters so that they may continue this in the family name. I currently am thirty three years old and still very passionate about my business. I feel that with my strong business ethics we will have you as part of our family for a long time.
